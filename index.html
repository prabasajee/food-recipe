    Self-Care Tips

    Mindful Breathing

    Take 5 deep breaths, inhaling through your nose and exhaling through your mouth. Focus only on your breath.

    Short Walk

    Even a 10-minute walk outside can boost your mood and clear your mind.

    Gratitude List

    Write down 3 things you're grateful for today, no matter how small.

    Digital Detox

    Take 30 minutes away from screens. Try reading, drawing, or just sitting quietly.

    Hydrate

    Drink a glass of water. Dehydration can affect your mood and energy levels.

    Positive Affirmation

    Repeat to yourself: "I am enough. My feelings are valid. This moment will pass."
